Our Story

A Beautiful
Experience

Imaj Hair Salon is a full service beauty salon that opened in 2006 on Connecticut Avenue, just a block from Cleveland Park Metro. Our owners take pride in delivering exceptional, high quality hair and nail services in a welcoming environment.

Our stylists and beauticians are trained, certified, and highly experienced. Since our focus is beauty, we feel responsible for making every client feel and look their absolute best — every visit.
